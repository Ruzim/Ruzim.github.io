Profile

PhD, University of Rochester, 2012
Dr. Yan's research interest is generally in the area of high energy density physics (HEDP). In particular, he is interested in exciting plasma physics topics relevant to inertial confinement fusion (ICF) and astrophysics. He uses large-scale Paricle-in-Cell (PIC) and has been developing fluid models to help understanding different levels of physical details in these highly nonlinear areas of physics.
